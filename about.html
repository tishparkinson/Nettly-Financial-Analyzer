What it is

Quick Exhale is a calm brain dump tool: a guided session with prompts and brain dump ideas, capture like a simple brain dump journal, then choose your best brain dump priorities—exactly three—and schedule time blocks, often into your calendar.

How it works

  1. Unload with rotating prompts (type or paper).
  2. Pick three things that would make today feel doable.
  3. Assign start time and duration for each.

Free and Pro

The core flow is free to use in your browser. Pro (monthly, billed by Stripe) adds calendar export and optional cloud backup of your saved session data.
