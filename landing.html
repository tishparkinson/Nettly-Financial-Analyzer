Questions

Do I have to connect my bank account?

No — Nettly never asks for your bank login or account credentials. You paste transaction text from your bank or card statement, and everything is processed right in your browser.

Where is my financial data stored?

On your device only. Nettly doesn't store your transaction history on any server — that's what makes it private. Download a snapshot file to pick up where you left off next time.

Will it judge my spending?

No, and that's a deliberate design choice — not a slogan. Nettly shows you information — patterns, trends, options — and lets you decide what to do with it. No categories are "bad," no numbers are "shameful."

What if I have irregular or multiple sources of income?

Nettly detects your actual pay pattern from your transactions — weekly, biweekly, monthly, or irregular — and adjusts its comparisons accordingly, rather than assuming everyone gets paid the same way.

Can I use it with a partner or household?

Yes — couples/household mode lets you track separate accounts for two people with a combined view, or filter to see just your own picture.
