💡 Tip: Download transactions since the last 3 months from each account. 2 months is the bare minimum for spotting recurring bills reliably — 3 gives real buffer if a bill lands a few days late or you miss one payment. Paste them one account at a time, then we'll walk you through setting your Needs.

    Tags

    Pick a preset or type your own — apply to recent transactions below.

    ▸ Ideas for what to track with tags
    • What a trip actually cost: tag every purchase from a trip (e.g. "Utah Trip") to see the real total — check the box below to also exclude it from your normal monthly average.
    • Who's spending what: tag by name (e.g. "Sarah," "Mike") to see individual spending inside shared finances — no separate accounts needed.
    • Cost per kid: tag by child (e.g. "Emma Clothes," "Jake Activities") to track what each one costs over time.
    • Joint vs. personal: a "Joint" tag and a "Personal" tag can separate household spending from individual discretionary spending.
