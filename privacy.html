What Quick Exhale is

The Site offers a browser-based tool to help users unload thoughts, choose priorities, and plan time blocks. A free tier runs mostly in your device. A Pro subscription (monthly, processed by Stripe) adds features such as calendar-related export and optional cloud backup of stored session data tied to your account.

Why Netlify does not “host” your subscription page

Netlify hosts our Site and runs our serverless code. It does not process card payments or provide the subscription management UI. Stripe provides the secure Customer Billing Portal (hosted on Stripe’s domain) after we create a portal session from our API. That matches common patterns: your previous project likely linked to Stripe’s portal, possibly via a Netlify Function.
